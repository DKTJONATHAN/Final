### Key Validation Requirements:

1. **Frontmatter Fields**:
   - ✅ `title`: 1-60 characters
   - ✅ `description`: 20-160 characters
   - ✅ `publishDate`: Valid date (YYYY-MM-DD recommended)
   - ✅ `tags`: Array of strings (optional)
   - ✅ `coverImage`: Object with `src` and `alt` (optional)

2. **Automatic Processing**:
   - Tags will be deduplicated and lowercased
   - Dates will be coerced to Date objects
   - Draft status defaults to false

3. **Best Practices**:
   - Keep title under 60 chars
   - Description between 20-160 chars
   - Use relative paths for images (store in `public/` folder)
   - Include alt text for all images
